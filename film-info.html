1915 Legend Of The Gurkhas is a feature film created by SHY Productions, directed by award-winning filmmaker Pradeep Shahi. The principal photography took place from November 2019 to March 2020 in England, United Kingdom. The budget was approx. £38500, from which most were via crowdfunding & sponsors. The cast and crew worked on a voluntary basis and all the profit from the movie goes to the Gurkha Welfare Trust.



This movie is a homage to our forefathers, in whose peril, WE RISE.
